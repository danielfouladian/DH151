Introduction

Daniel Fouladian is a senior pursuing a B.A in Psychology with a minor in Digital Humanities at the University of California, Los Angeles (2022). He continues to succeed academically as he was born deaf and wears a Cochlear Implant to hear. He hopes he can inspire many more to overcome their obstacles and hardships. In his free time, Fouladian loves playing soccer and basketball with friends, working with graphic design, and playing video games.

Fouladian's goals for this course are to refresh his coding abilities in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as he was first exposed to the languages in high school. He aims to be the best bruin that can code in the three languages and impress Yoh in each assignment.
